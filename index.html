Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 February 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the mobile game application "Khinkali Hunter" (the "App").

Overview

  • The App can be used offline for core gameplay.
  • Some features may require internet connection (for example, leaderboards or cloud features).
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • When online features are used, some data may be processed by third-party services (see below).

Information We Collect

We do not ask you to provide personal information like your name, phone number, or precise location to play the App.

If you contact us by email, we receive your email address and message content and use it only to respond to you.

Online Features and PlayFab

The App uses PlayFab to provide optional online functionality such as leaderboards and related services. When you use these features, PlayFab may process certain data needed to operate them.

The data involved may include:

  • App-generated player identifiers (for example, an anonymous ID or PlayFab player ID).
  • Gameplay and progress data relevant to the online feature (for example, scores submitted to leaderboards).
  • Device and technical information commonly required for online services (for example, device identifiers, IP address, app version, and diagnostics).

This data is used to provide and secure online features, prevent abuse, and maintain service performance.

Data Sharing

We share data only as necessary to operate the App's optional online features through PlayFab. We do not sell your information.

Retention

If online features are used, online-related data (for example, leaderboard entries) may be stored on PlayFab for as long as needed to provide those features, unless you request deletion where applicable and technically possible.

Your Choices

  • You can use the App offline and avoid optional online features.
  • If you do not want leaderboard or cloud-related processing, do not use the online features.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Security

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We take reasonable measures to protect the information processed for the App, and rely on trusted providers for online features.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

Your use of the Application is also governed by our Terms and Conditions.

Contact
